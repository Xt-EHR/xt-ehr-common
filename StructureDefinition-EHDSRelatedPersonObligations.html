Logical Model: Related person obligations

Official URL: http://www.xt-ehr.eu/fhir/models/StructureDefinition/EHDSRelatedPersonObligations Version: 0.4.0
Draft as of 2026-03-13 Computable Name: EHDSRelatedPersonObligations

Obligations for the logical model for related person.

Usages:

  • This Logical Model Profile is not used by any profiles in this Implementation Guide

You can also check for usages in the FHIR IG Statistics

NameFlagsCard.TypeDescription & Constraints    Filter:
Bindings
Constraints
Obligations
EHDSRelatedPerson 0..* EHDSRelatedPerson Related person model
personalIdentifier O 0..* Identifier An identifier of the related person that is unique within a defined scope (e.g. national ID number). Multiple identifiers could be provided.
ObligationsActor
SHOULD:able-to-populate Producer
name O 0..* EHDSHumanName Name associated with the person.
ObligationsActor
SHOULD:able-to-populate Producer
subject O 1..1 EHDSPatient The patient in relation to whom the related person is defined.
ObligationsActor
SHALL:able-to-populate Producer
relationship O 0..1 CodeableConcept Relationship between a patient and a contact person for that patient. This includes relatives, guardians, caring persons etc.
Binding Description: (preferred): HL7 RoleCode
ObligationsActor
SHALL:able-to-populate Producer
address 0..* EHDSAddress Mailing and home or office address
telecom O 0..* EHDSTelecom Telecommunication contact information (addresses) of the person.
ObligationsActor
SHOULD:able-to-populate Producer
